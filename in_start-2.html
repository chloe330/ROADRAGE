Specialty Coffee

Specialty coffee is produced in small quantities with a focus on high-quality beans and unique flavors. It features careful sourcing and processing and is found in dedicated coffee shops and specialty retailers.

Commercial Coffee

Commercial coffee is mass-produced by major companies using lower-quality beans to keep costs low and maintain consistency. It's widely available in supermarkets, coffee shops, and restaurants.
